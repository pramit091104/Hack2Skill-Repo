# NutriSmart AI Design System

## Theme
Clean, calm, and minimal. Lots of whitespace, subtle borders, and no heavy drop shadows. The design should feel airy, fresh, and health-oriented.

## Typography
Primary Font: 'Inter', sans-serif. 
Use light/regular weights for large headings to keep them elegant. Use medium weights for interactive elements and buttons. Avoid heavy, thick fonts.

## Colors
- Primary: #4CAF50 (Fresh minimal green)
- Secondary: #F0F0F0 (Soft light gray for subtle accents)
- Background: #FFFFFF (Pure white)
- Surface: #FAFAFA (Extremely light off-white for slight separation)
- Text Primary: #222222 (Soft dark gray for high readability, avoid harsh #000)
- Text Secondary: #777777 (Medium gray for less important info)

## Components
- Buttons: Minimal, flat with slight rounded corners (6px). No heavy shadows. Primary buttons are solid green with white text. Secondary buttons use soft gray or are simply outlined.
- Inputs: Clean, spacious inputs with faint borders (#EAEAEA). High padding for an airy feel.
- Cards: Flat with a very subtle 1px border (#EEEEEE). Avoid heavy depth or 3D effects.
- Navigation: Simple textual links with ample spacing. Active states highlighted with subtle primary color text or a minimal underline.
